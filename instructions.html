how to play

welcome! glad to have you here! hover over if you want to know how to play :)

  • instructions

    flip the cards pair by pair until you find
    a matching one. each pair turning counts as one try.
    you are limited on time and number of tries.
    you win if you find all matching pairs!

  • modes

    easy mode: 30 tries for 3 minutes
    normal mode: 20 tries for 2 minutes
    hard mode: 15 tries for 1 minute.

  • share the game

    you can also share this game
    to a person of your choice.
